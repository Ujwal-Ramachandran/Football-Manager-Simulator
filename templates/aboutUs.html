About Us

Football is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each and a referee. It is the most popular sport in the world, with about 3.5 billion fanatics. With a vast fandom, arises big fights on which is the best/ideal team in a league or who is the best player in that league. This project will bring an end to this endless battle.

This website is targeted to those hardcore football fans who wish to satisfy their curiosity and settle any debate on which player really is the best. The website aims to provide a clear graphic, which helps football lovers to interpret and make reasonable assessments about real world football players.

Football Manager Simulator is a passion project for our group, and we hope this Website can be enjoyed by both Football fanatics and an average user alike.
